Chess Reimagined -- ShatteredCrown

Game Basics

Shattered crown is a modified version of chess where each piece has their own hit points, attack damage, and special ability. Instead of an immediate capture, pieces hit their target and return to their original spot.

On a fatal shot, you will be prompted to type a sequence to letters to complete the capture.

As mentioned earlier, each piece has their own unique abilities with a certain cooldown. The King has some one time, game changing abilities that you may choose from at the beginning of the game.



Piece HP Attack Ability Description Demo
♙ Pawn 40 10 Strength in Numbers--Adjacent friendly pawns gains +1 HP for each pawn at the end of a turn
♖ Rook 50 20 Hammer Time--Attacks also hit the enemy behind the target
♘ Knight 60 12 Hippity Hoppity--Attacks can chain to 3 enemies
♗ Bishop 30 20 Pope-ing Off--Heal all friendly pieces in a 3x3 area by 7 HP
♕ Queen 100 25 Stunning Beauty--Deals extra 10% damage proportional to the targets health and stuns the target
♔ King 35 4 You may choose of of the four abilities at the start of the game. They can only be used once per game. See below.
